I did BS (Hons) in Information Technology from University of Education, Lahore (Dera Ghazi Khan Campus) in 2021. I completed my MS in Computer Science from COMSATS University Islamabad (CUI) under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Nadeem Javaid in September 2023, and was part of the Communication over Sensors (ComSens) Research Lab from February to September 2023. My research interests cover data science, healthcare systems, data analytics, and machine and deep learning. I currently serve as a Lecturer in the Department of Computing and Intelligent Systems at the University of Rawalpindi (UOR), Rawalpindi 46000, Pakistan.
